      Design Principles

      What this dashboard is built to measure

      The underlying framework remains visible, without shipping the original screenshot in the product.

      • Shared definitions and meaningful benchmarks
      • Hiring risk in an era of AI and regulation
      • Time to productivity and value per hire
      • Internal mobility, leadership pipeline and skills coverage

      The dashboard is intentionally split between operational hiring metrics and strategic workforce signals, so recruitment can be discussed in terms of delivery, risk and business value.

      Operational clarity

      Track time to offer, time to start, funnel conversion and cost per starter with consistent definitions.

      Candidate visibility

      Use search and filtering to move from portfolio view to named candidates, stages and skills.

      Business outcomes

      Make room for quality of hire, productivity, value per hire and strategic skills coverage.
